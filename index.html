Your host

Ann Bondarchuk is a founder and CEO of Deliberate Choice Christian Counseling and Coaching. She is an Executive Christian Counselor and Coach, Supervisor and Lecturer.

Ann helps executives, business owners and full-time ministers to prevent and overcome burnout working through its roots so they could gain control back, restore their ability to enjoy life and not miss out on what matters in life for them the most. Ann helps them to make sure their past doesn't destroy their present and future.

Ann has conducted individual, family and group counseling and coaching for the last 16 years. Ann works with a great team of coaches and counselors. Ann works with burnout, childhood traumas, deprivation, sexual abuse, domestic violence, stress, anxiety, depression, grief, loss, panic attacks, marriage and family issues. Ann also provides coaching in the area of life and work balance, mindfulness, corporate setting conflicts, spiritual formation, personal effectiveness.
